Our Reservation Policies


- We have a 15 minute grace period. Please call us if you are running later than 15 minutes after your reservation time.
- We may contact you about this reservation, so please ensure your email and phone number are up to date.
- Your table will be reserved for 1 hour 30 minutes for parties of up to 2; 2 hours for parties of up to 6; and 2 hours 30 minutes for parties of 7+.
- Please note, changes of time and party size are subject to availability.
- A gratuity of 18% will be added to the bill for parties of 6 or more. We cannot accommodate split bills, however multiple payments are accepted.
- When booking reservations please include children & infants in the party size.
- Kindly call us during business hours to request an increase in party hours.
- For parties of 10+, please email us at info@aamara.ca.
